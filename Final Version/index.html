春眠不覚暁
処処聞啼鳥
夜来風雨声
花落知多少

Come spring, the rays of dawn no longer wake
I hear the song the birds surrounding make
Then last night's tempest suddenly recall
And wonder whether fell the flowers all

Summer Mood
蘇舜欽
Su Shunqin

別院深深夏箪清
石榴開遍透簾明
樹陰満地日当午
夢覚流鶯時一声

On mat bamboo reclining deep within a yard serene
The sun ascends to noon, the ground awash in pools of shade
My dream dispelled with warbling by a nightingale unseen

等閒三伏後
独臥此高丘
林風爽帶秋
声名何出
吟詠亦堪休
自許紅塵外
雲溪好漱流

I live in ease once past the dog-star days
These wooded heights alone inhabiting
The forest winds a breath autumnal bring
What need to leave and seek for fame inane?
To ever odes and verse compose and sing?
Apart I stand from all the dirt mundane
Content my mouth to cleanse at mountain spring

禁寒惜暖十余春
往事回頭倍愴神
幾度登楼親視膳
掲開幃幕已無人

I kept out cold and kept in warmth for winters more than ten
It multiplies my sorrow to recall the days back when
How many times with tray of food in hand I climbed the stair!
And now to lift the curtain 'round the bed, and no one's there!
